Providing people the opportunity to share their belongings to people who need them!

THE PROBLEM

Have you ever planned a special once-in-a-lifetime event where you invited a bunch of your friends and at the very last minute forgot something integral to the event? Something like: chairs, a big tent, or tables. You didn't want to buy those items because you know you weren't going to need them again for a long time. So what's the solution???

THE SOLUTION

The problem of not having what you specifically need in moments of rarity is exactly what Lendr is here to solve. With this product your able to search for items you need and rent them out from your fellow neighbors on a special occasion basis. On the flipside, if you have items you want to lend out for people in need, Lendr is the perfect place to host those items. Click the Sign Up Now button to get started today!
